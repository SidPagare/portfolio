UX case study

Trackity

Trackity is a governance app that provides managing authorities with real time locations of their field and office staff and allows them to monitor their locations during working hours to ensure healthy attendance.

The Challenge

The Municipal Departments have as much as 6000+ staff out of which around 94% is field staff. The staff is often assigned tasks they need to carry out in the field.

One of the biggest issues authorities face is managing these personals and making sure the staff is addressing the issues by being on the site.

My Role

  • Research
  • User Persona
  • Empathy Map
  • User Interviews
  • Wireframes

The Solution

A smartphone app that monitors real time location of on field & office staff to ensure prompt attendance.

Design Process

1. Empathize

2. Define

3. Ideate

4. Design

Empathize

I conducted simple User Interviews & tried gathering as much information as possible to gain insights on pain points. Multiple managing authorities were interviewed for broad understanding of the issue.

Here are some of the questions I framed for conducting the interviews

Empathy Mapping

After gaining some insight on the issue, I used Empathy Mapping to deeply understand the user and get a clear picture of user needs that would then drive the design decisions.

Define

The empathasise phase helped me to gain insight of target user and their needs. At this phase I analysed all the information I gathered from conducting interviews and started defining the core problem

To focus on target user and priorotize their needs I created User Personas

The "HMW" Framework

The HMW (How Might We) framework helped me to reframe the users problem and provided a headstart for launching a brainstorm

How might we make communication between authorities and workers more streamline?

How might we make it easier for authorities to fact check staff's work?

How might we make sure every issue is addressed by the on field staff?

How might we make monitoring of employees more efficient?

Ideate

At this phase I had clear understanding of the target user, their needs and the issue itself. It was time to start thinking about all the solutions. My team members and I started brainstorming and we came up with multiple solutions. Considering all the factors like fesibility, sustainability & effectiveness of solution we shortlisted one solution

Proposed Solution

  • Geofence Technology allowed us to make sure that the employees were at their designated place during office hours
  • No employees are allowed to update the work status for assigned jobs unless they are inside the task zone.
